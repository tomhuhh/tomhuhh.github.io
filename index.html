Ph.D. Candidate · Department of Animal Science · Cornell University

Haowen Hu

Building tools that help dairy systems measure, model, and improve their environmental footprint — from barn sensors to whole-farm simulation.

I research whole-farm environmental and systems modeling for US dairy production, with a focus on the Ruminant Farm Systems (RuFaS) model: quantifying ammonia and greenhouse-gas emissions, refining models with field sensor data, and translating that work into decision-support tools farmers and advisors can actually use.
